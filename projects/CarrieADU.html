Carrie’s Garage-to-ADU Conversion Roadmap

Portland 2026 Code Compliant

Project Overview

This roadmap outlines the permitting, construction, and inspection process for converting an existing garage into a legal Accessory Dwelling Unit (ADU) in Portland, Oregon.

The project follows an Owner-Builder permit structure, where the homeowner acts as the permit holder for certain regulated trades, while Halli Construction completes all structural work, finish work, and final build-out.

Phase 1: Administrative Intake

DevHub Setup

The Owner establishes an account with the City of Portland’s DevHub portal.

https://devhub.portlandoregon.gov/

Owner-Builder Acknowledgments

Where required, the Owner executes administrative acknowledgments allowing permits to be issued under an Owner-Builder framework.

Owner Doing Work / Owner-Builder Statement (PDF)

SDC Waiver (Major Cost Savings)

System Development Charges (SDCs) for ADUs typically total $12,000–$20,000.

The City of Portland waives these fees when the Owner signs and records a covenant agreeing not to operate the ADU as a short-term rental for 10 years.

SDC Waiver Program Information

Critical Requirement — SDC Waiver:
The SDC Waiver Covenant must be signed, notarized, and recorded with the county. Proof of recording is required before permits can be issued.

Phase 2: Construction & Rough-In

Structural & Enclosure Work

Halli Construction completes all foundation, framing, exterior walls, roofing, and windows, bringing the structure to a weather-tight shell.

Trade Rough-In (Owner-Permitted)

Electrical, plumbing, and mechanical systems proceed under permits issued to the Owner, coordinated to align with structural and finish requirements.

Rough-In Inspections

Required inspections must be approved prior to insulation or wall closure.

Phase 3: Interior Completion & Occupancy

Insulation, Drywall & Finish Work

Halli Construction completes insulation, drywall, trim, cabinetry, fixtures, and all final interior finishes.

Final Inspection & Certificate of Occupancy

The City conducts final inspections and issues a Certificate of Occupancy upon approval.

Simplified Project Timeline (Roles Identified)

  1. DevHub account creation — Owner

  3. Permit application submission — Owner / Halli Construction
  4. Structural construction — Halli Construction
  5. Trade rough-in (Owner-permitted) — Owner
  6. Rough-in inspections — Owner / City
