Trade Permits Consulting on code-compliant layout and circuit loads. Pulls Electrical, Plumbing, and Mechanical permits through DevHub.

4. Key 2026 Regulatory Notes

Owner-Builder Residency Requirement:
Oregon law allows homeowners to perform their own electrical and plumbing work only if they own and reside in the unit.
Egress Compliance:
Sleeping areas must include a compliant egress window (minimum 5.7 sq ft clear opening; sill ≤ 44").
Fire Separation:
Garages within 3 feet of a property line require a 1-hour fire-rated wall assembly.
