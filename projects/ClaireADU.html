Phase 1: Administrative Intake

DevHub Account

The Owner must create a profile on the City of Portland’s Development Hub (DevHub). This is the official system for plan uploads, corrections, approvals, inspection scheduling, and permit payments.

Access Development Hub PDX

Owner Doing Work Statement

Because the Owner is performing the electrical and plumbing labor, Oregon law requires a signed affidavit confirming that the Owner holds title to the property and will personally perform the work.

Download Owner Doing Work Statement (PDF)

SDC Waiver & Covenant

Portland normally assesses System Development Charges (SDCs) totaling approximately $12,000–$20,000 for a new dwelling unit.

These fees are waived for Accessory Dwelling Units if the Owner signs and records a covenant agreeing not to use the unit as a short-term rental (such as Airbnb) for a minimum of 10 years.

Process: Notarization → County recording → Permit issuance
