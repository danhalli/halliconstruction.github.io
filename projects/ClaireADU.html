Garage-to-ADU Conversion Roadmap

Location: Portland, Oregon  |  Target Code Year: 2026

Phase 1: Administrative Intake

DevHub Account

The Owner must create a profile on the City of Portland’s DevHub portal. This platform is the official system for plan uploads, corrections, approvals, and permit payments.

Owner Doing Work Statement

Because the Owner is performing the electrical and plumbing labor, Oregon law requires a signed affidavit confirming:

  • The Owner holds title to the property
  • The Owner will personally perform the trade work
  • No separate trade contractor license is required

SDC Waiver & Covenant (Critical Cost Savings)

Portland normally assesses System Development Charges (SDCs) totaling approximately $12,000–$20,000 for a new dwelling unit.

These fees are waived entirely for ADUs if the Owner signs and records a covenant agreeing not to use the unit as a short-term rental (e.g., Airbnb) for 10 years.
