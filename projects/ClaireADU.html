Garage-to-ADU Conversion Roadmap

Portland, Oregon · 2026 Code Cycle

Phase 1: Administrative Intake

DevHub Account

The Owner must create a profile on the City of Portland’s DevHub portal. This system is used for plan uploads, corrections, approvals, and payments.

Owner Doing Work Statement

Because the Owner is performing the electrical and plumbing labor, Oregon law requires a signed affidavit confirming ownership and personal performance of the work.

SDC Waiver & Covenant

Portland normally assesses System Development Charges (SDCs) of approximately $12,000–$20,000 for a new dwelling unit.

These fees are waived for ADUs if the Owner signs a covenant agreeing not to use the unit as a short-term rental for 10 years.

Process: Notarization → County recording → Permit issuance
