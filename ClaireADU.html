3. Estimated City Fees (Portland 2026)

  • Building Permit & Plan Review: ~$1,500 – $2,500 (Varies by project valuation)
  • Electrical Permit (Owner-Builder): ~$165
  • Plumbing Permit (Owner-Builder): ~$350 – $500
  • Mechanical Permit (Owner-Builder): ~$150 – $250
  • System Development Charges (SDCs): ~$0 (if SDC waiver is executed) :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}
